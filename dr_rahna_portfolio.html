Research & Scholarly Work

Integrating Brain-Based Learning with Transformative Pedagogy in Science: Impact on Academic Achievement, Holistic Development, and Global Citizenship
International Education and Research Journal (IERJ) · 2026 · Corresponding Author · ISSN 2455-295X
Three-year longitudinal action research (Grade 12, n=72, JSS Private School Dubai, 2021–2024). Demonstrated consistent CBSE Biology +8.79%, Chemistry +7.80%, Physics +6.27% examination gains alongside transformative improvements in self-efficacy, metacognition, and global citizenship orientation across 72 students.
NeuroTransform: Brain-Based Learning Integrated with Transformative Pedagogy
International Conference Presentation · Dual-presenter session with Ms. Smitha Vakkayil · JSS Private School Dubai
Presented the NeuroTransform model linking neuroscience (Caine & Caine, Jensen) with Mezirow and Freire's transformative frameworks; demonstrated implementation pathways and longitudinal student outcome data across three Science subjects.
12+ Peer-Reviewed Publications — Molecular Biology, Genetics & Biotechnology
